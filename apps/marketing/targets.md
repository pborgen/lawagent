Named, link-backed targets for the outreach/distribution ideas. Researched
2026-06-01 via the deep-research harness (25 claims verified, mostly primary
sources). **Verify links before sending — sites change.**

> **Priority read:** the open-right-now submission paths are **Stanford AI+A2J
> Google Form**, **Legaltech Hub free vendor listing**, and cold tips to
> **LawSites/LawNext, Artificial Lawyer, Above the Law**. The strongest
> *topical* fits (A2J framing is an advantage) are **Stanford AI+A2J**,
